SELECTION OF POCKET MONSTERS

Pocket monsters have distinct characters such as life, attack and special attack mode for players to choose.

POKECT MONSTER CREATED BY COMPUTER

The pocket monster controlled by computer will be randomly selected.

GAME WINNER

The winner will be the pocket monster who has the positive health point after combat damage step if the opponent pocket monster has no more than zero health point.

GAME TIE

This will happen if two pocket monsters both have negative health point after combat damage step.

BATTLE STARTS

The turn player declares that they are beginning their combat phase. The player chooses the attack mode before its combat phase.

BATTLE DAMAGE STEP

The attack mode of pocket monster controlled by computer will be randomly selected to attack. Pocket monsters deal damage equal to their power at the time of combat phase.

BATTLE END STEP

Both pocket monsters will check their health point at the end of each combat phase. If one of them has negative health point and the other has positive health point , the winner will be the one with positive health point. If both of them have positive health point, the combat continues to the second round until there is a winner. If both of the have negative health point at the end of combat phase, there is a tie game.
